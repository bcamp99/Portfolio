This is a mock-up design crafted with Figma, showcasing the first phase of the Tears of the Kingdom Companion App. It emphasizes the streaming component, presenting different streams of the Zelda TOTK game, clips, and streamer pages, all meticulously arranged to optimize user experience.

In creating this design, I laid the groundwork by crafting basic wireframes within Figma. Once these structures were in place, I shifted to the design tab to infuse the project with intricate details. This entailed the meticulous creation of usernames, profile images, and the integration of captions/comments facilitated by handy plugins. To enrich the visual appeal, I curated images sourced from diverse corners of the internet. This iterative process has been immensely instructive, and I'm enthusiastic about delving into future phases of this app concept.
