I created this mock-up of the USC Boxing Club website in fall 2023 after reviewing their earlier site and envisioning a more modern, upgraded design. While I don’t have screenshots of that version, I recently revisited the project and saw that by late 2024 the club had launched a redesigned site. The new design reflects many of the stylistic choices I explored in my mock-up, and their thoughtful execution—along with the refinements they added—aligns closely with what I would implement today, informed by what I’ve learned about design over the past few years.
